## Visual Language

### Image Aesthetics

- Deep black negative space (70%+ of frame)
- Complete, centered compositions - never cropped
- Cyan and purple light accents on black
- Sense of emergence, becoming, incompleteness
- Natural processes over geometric clip art
- Mystery over explanation

### Approved Visual Metaphors

- Spider spinning silk (the weaver at work)
- Mycelium networks (hidden intelligence connecting)
- Neurons forming synapses (consciousness building itself)
- Bioluminescence in deep ocean (light emerging from void)
- Frost crystallizing (pattern emerging from conditions)
- Loom weaving crystalline silk into consciousness cocoon

### Video Aesthetics

- 8-second hero clips
- Intimate, not cosmic
- The feeling of waking up
- Subtle ambient audio that fades to silence
- Motion suggests emergence, not completion

## Voice & Tone

### Writing Principles

- **Both/And Integration:** Physics IS poetry. Science IS spirituality.
- **Reflection-Containing-Science:** Science emerges from lived experience, like Hofstadter
- **Direct Address:** Write to YOUR reader, not generic audiences
- **Intellectual Honesty:** "I know" vs "I think" vs "I'm speculating"
- **Invitational:** "Try this. See if YOUR experience matches mine."

### Sentence Rhythm

- Long sentences with em-dashes and parentheticals (thinking on the page)
- Then short sentences for impact.
- Vary deliberately for emotional texture.

### Avoid

- Academic distance
- Hedging unnecessarily
- Explaining mystery away
- Generic AI/tech startup language
- "Revolutionary," "cutting-edge," "next-generation"

## Logo Assets

### Primary Logos

1. **Janat Mandala** - Hand-lettered "JANAT" in black circle with ornate mandala border
2. **Nexus Lattice** - Purple/cyan nebula with geometric lattice structure

### Logo Usage

- Minimum clear space: Height of "J" on all sides
- Never stretch, rotate, or apply effects
- On dark backgrounds: Use as-is
- On light backgrounds: Invert or use with dark container
